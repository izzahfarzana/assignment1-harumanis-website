About Me

I'm a farmer and entrepreneur from Perlis. I started this Harumanis business in 2020 by planting 40 trees and gradually developed it into a family-run business.

Through dedication and patience, I have successfully contributed to the production of high-quality Harumanis mangoes.

About Harumanis Mango

Harumanis is a premium mango from Perlis, known for its fragrant aroma, smooth texture, and very sweet taste.

It is available only once a year (March–June) and grows best in Perlis climate.

Proper care is important to maintain the quality of the trees and fruits.
